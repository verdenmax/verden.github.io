    题目一

    \[ A = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & -2 \\ -1 & 2 \\ \end{pmatrix} \]
    \[ A^TA - \lambda I = \begin{pmatrix} 2- \lambda & -4 \\ -4 & 8 - \lambda \\ \end{pmatrix} \rightarrow \begin{pmatrix} 2- \lambda & -4 \\ -4 + \frac{(2-\lambda)(8- \lambda)}{4} & 0 \\ \end{pmatrix} \]

    那么 \(A^TA - \lambda I\) 是一个奇异矩阵,当 \((2-\lambda)(8-\lambda) - 16 = 0 = \lambda ^2 - 10 \lambda\)，即 \(\lambda = 0\) 或 \(\lambda = 10\)。

    其中：

    \(||A||_F = \sqrt{1^2 + (-2)^2 + (-1)^2 + 2 ^2} = \sqrt{10}\)

    \(||A||_1 = max_j \sum_i {|a_{i,j}|} = 4\)

    \(||A||_2 = \sqrt{\lambda_{max}} = \sqrt{10}\)

    \(||A||_{\infty} = max_i \sum_j {|a_{i,j}|} = 2\)

    \[ B = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & 1 & 0\\ 0 & 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 & 0 \\ \end{pmatrix} \]

    \(B^TB = I\)，所以 \(B^T B\) 的特征值为 \(\lambda = 1\) 。

    其中：

    \(||B||_F = \sqrt{ 1^2 + 1^2 + 1^2 } = \sqrt{3}\)

    \(||B||_1 = max_j \sum_i {|b_{i,j}|} = 1\)

    \(||B||_2 = \sqrt{\lambda_{max}} = 1\)

    \(||B||_{\infty} = max_i \sum_j {|b_{i,j}|} = 1\)

    \[ C = \begin{pmatrix} 4 & -2 & 4\\ -2 & 1 & -2 \\ 4 & -2 & 4 \\ \end{pmatrix} \]
    \[ C^TC - \lambda I = \begin{pmatrix} 36 - \lambda & -18 & 36 \\ -18 & 9 - \lambda & -18 \\ 36 & -18 & 36 - \lambda \\ \end{pmatrix} \rightarrow \begin{pmatrix} - \lambda & -2 \lambda & 0 \\ -18 & 9 - \lambda & -18 \\ 0 & -2 \lambda & - \lambda \\ \end{pmatrix}\\ \rightarrow \begin{pmatrix} - \lambda & -2 \lambda & 0 \\ -18 & 9 - \lambda & -18 \\ \lambda & -2 \lambda+ \frac{\lambda(\lambda - 9)}{18} & 0 \\ \end{pmatrix}\\ \rightarrow \begin{pmatrix} - \lambda & -2 \lambda & 0 \\ -18 & 9 - \lambda & -18 \\ 0 & -4 \lambda+ \frac{\lambda(\lambda - 9)}{18} & 0 \\ \end{pmatrix}\\ \]

    转化为求解\(\lambda ^2 (\frac{\lambda - 81}{18}) = 0\)，解得 \(\lambda = 0\)或 \(\lambda = 81\)

    其中：

    \(||C||_F = \sqrt{ 4^2 + (-2)^2 + 4^2 + (-2)^2 + 1 + (-2)^2 + 4^2 + (-2)^2 + 4^2} = \sqrt{81}\)

    \(||C||_1 = max_j \sum_i {|b_{i,j}|} = 10\)

    \(||C||_2 = \sqrt{\lambda_{max}} = \sqrt{10}\)

    \(||C||_{\infty} = max_i \sum_j {|b_{i,j}|} = 10\)

    题目二

    对于空间 \(R^{2\times 2}\),定义 \(<A,B> = trace(A^T B)\)

    (1) 说明满足内积定义：

    • \(<A,A> = trace(A^T A) = \sum_{i,j} a_{i,j}^2 \ge 0\)

    • \(<A,A> = 0\) 当切仅当 \(A = 0\)

    • \(<A,\alpha B> = trace(A^T \alpha B) = \alpha trace(A^T B) = \alpha <A,B>\)

    • \(<A, B + C> = trace(A^TB+ A^TC) = trace(A^TB) + trace(A^TC) = <A,B> + <A,C>\)

    • \(<A,B> = trace(A^T B) = \sum_{i,j} a_{ij} b_{ij} = trace(B^TA) = <B,A>\)

    (2) 证明下面的 \(\mathcal{B}\) 是向量空间 \(R^{2 \times 2}\)中的一组标准正交基

    \[ \mathcal{B}= \left \{ \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \begin{pmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0\\ \end{pmatrix} , \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \\ \end{pmatrix} , \frac{1}{2} \begin{pmatrix} 1 & -1 \\ 1 & 1 \\ \end{pmatrix} , \frac{1}{2} \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ -1 & 1\\ \end{pmatrix} \right \} \]

    可以记

    \[ B_1 = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \begin{pmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0\\ \end{pmatrix} , B_2 = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \\ \end{pmatrix} , B_3 = \frac{1}{2} \begin{pmatrix} 1 & -1 \\ 1 & 1 \\ \end{pmatrix} , B_4 = \frac{1}{2} \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ -1 & 1\\ \end{pmatrix} \]

    那么可以可以验证

    \[ <B_i,B_j> = \begin{cases} 1, i = j \\ 0, i \ne j \\ \end{cases} \]

    所以 \(\mathcal{B}\) 是标准正交集合。\(\mathcal{B}\) 有四个线性无关，所以是空间 \(R^{2\times 2}\) 的基。

    矩阵 \(A\) 在该组基下的傅里叶展开为：

    \[ A = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1\\ \end{pmatrix} \\ = <B_1,A> B_1 + <B_2,A> B_2 + <B_3,A> B_3 + <B_4,A> B_4 = \sqrt{2} B_1 + B_3 + B_4 \]

    题目三

    向量组

    \[ \left \{ x_1= \begin{pmatrix} 1\\ 0\\ 10^{-3}\\ \end{pmatrix} , x_2= \begin{pmatrix} 1\\ 0\\ 0\\ \end{pmatrix} , x_3= \begin{pmatrix} 1\\ 10^{-3}\\ 0\\ \end{pmatrix} \right \} \]

    先使用传统 Gram-Schmidt 进行正交化

    \[ u_1= \frac{x_1}{||x_1||} = \begin{pmatrix} 1\\ 0\\ 10^{-3}\\ \end{pmatrix} \]
    \[ u_2= \frac{x_2 - <x_2,u_1>u_1}{||x_2 - <x_2,u_1>u_1||} = \frac{ \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ \end{pmatrix} ^T - \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 & 10{-3} \\ \end{pmatrix} ^T } {||x_2 - <x_2,u_1>u_1||} \\ = \frac{ \begin{pmatrix} 0 & 0 & -10^{-3}\\ \end{pmatrix}^T } { || \begin{pmatrix} 0 & 0 & -10^{-3}\\ \end{pmatrix} ^T ||} = \begin{pmatrix} 0\\ 0\\ -1\\ \end{pmatrix} \]
    \[ u_3 = x_3 - <x_3,u_2>u_2 - <x_3,u_1>u_1 = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 10^{-3} \\ 0 \\ \end{pmatrix} - \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 0 \\ 10{-3} \\ \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} 0 \\ 10^{-3} \\ -10^{-3}\\ \end{pmatrix} ,\\ u_3 = \frac{u_3}{||u_3||} = \begin{pmatrix} 0 \\ 0.709 \\ -0.709\\ \end{pmatrix} \]

    因此使用传统的Gram-Schmit 正交化后结果为

    \[ \left \{ u_1= \begin{pmatrix} 1\\ 0\\ 10^{-3}\\ \end{pmatrix} , u_2= \begin{pmatrix} 0\\ 0\\ -1\\ \end{pmatrix} , u_3= \begin{pmatrix} 0\\ 0.709\\ -0.709\\ \end{pmatrix} \right \} \]

    使用修改后的Gram-Schmidt

    \[ k=1, f(||x_1||) = 1, {u_1,u_2,u_3} = {x_1,x_2,x_3} \]
    \[ k=2, u_1^T u_2 = 1, u_1^T u_3 = 1,\\ u_2 = u_2 - u_1^T u_2 u_1 = \begin{pmatrix} 0\\ 0\\ -10^{-3}\\ \end{pmatrix}\\ u_3 = u_3 - u_1^T u_3 u_1 = \begin{pmatrix} 0\\ 10^{-3}\\ -10^{-3}\\ \end{pmatrix}\\ u_2 = \frac{u_2}{||u_2||} = \begin{pmatrix} 0\\ 0\\ -1\\ \end{pmatrix}\\ \]
    \[ k=3, u_2^T u_3 = 10^{-3}, u_3 = u_3 - u_2^T u_3 u_2 = \begin{pmatrix} 0\\ 10^{-3}\\ 0\\ \end{pmatrix}\\ u_3 = \frac{u_3}{||u_3||} \begin{pmatrix} 0\\ 1\\ 0\\ \end{pmatrix}\\ \]

    所以使用修改后的Gram-Schmidt结果为

    \[ \left \{ u_1= \begin{pmatrix} 1\\ 0\\ 10^{-3}\\ \end{pmatrix} , u_2= \begin{pmatrix} 0\\ 0\\ -1\\ \end{pmatrix} , u_3= \begin{pmatrix} 0\\ 1\\ 0\\ \end{pmatrix} \right \} \]

    \[ A= \begin{pmatrix} \frac{1+i}{\sqrt{3}} & \frac{1+i}{\sqrt{6}} \\ \frac{i}{\sqrt{3}} & \frac{-2i}{\sqrt{6}} \\ \end{pmatrix} \]
    \[ A^*= \begin{pmatrix} \frac{1-i}{\sqrt{3}} & \frac{-i}{\sqrt{3}}\\ \frac{1-i}{\sqrt{6}} & \frac{2i}{\sqrt{6}} \\ \end{pmatrix} \]

    则

    \[ A^* A = \begin{pmatrix} \frac{1-i}{\sqrt{3}} \frac{1+i}{\sqrt{3}} + \frac{-i}{\sqrt{3}} \frac{i}{\sqrt{3}} & \frac{1-i}{\sqrt{3}} \frac{1+i}{\sqrt{6}} + \frac{-i}{\sqrt{3}} \frac{-2i}{\sqrt{6}} \\ \frac{1-i}{\sqrt{6}} \frac{1+i}{\sqrt{3}} + \frac{2i}{\sqrt{6}} \frac{i}{\sqrt{3}} & \frac{1-i}{\sqrt{6}} \frac{1+i}{\sqrt{6}} + \frac{2i}{\sqrt{6}} \frac{-2i}{\sqrt{6}} & \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \\ \end{pmatrix} \]

    所以矩阵是酉矩阵。

    题目六

    \[ A = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & -20 & -14\\ 3 & 27 & -4 \\ 4 & 11 & -2 \\ \end{pmatrix} \]

    使用 Given reduction 方法找到正交矩阵 \(P\)，使得 \(PA=T\)，\(T\) 为上三角。

    \[ P_{12}= \begin{pmatrix} 0 & 1 & 0 \\ -1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \\ \end{pmatrix}, P_{13}= \begin{pmatrix} \frac{3}{5} & 0 & \frac{4}{5}\\ 0 & 1 & 0 \\ -\frac{4}{5} & 0 & \frac{3}{5}\\ \end{pmatrix}, \]

    则：

    \[ P_{13}P_{12}A = \begin{pmatrix} 5 & 25 & -4 \\ 0 & 20 & 14 \\ 0 & -15 & 2 \\ \end{pmatrix}, \]
    \[ P_{23}= \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & \frac{4}{5} & -\frac{3}{5} \\ 0 & \frac{3}{5} & \frac{4}{5} \\ \end{pmatrix}, \]

    则：

    \[ P_{23} P_{13} P_{12} A = \begin{pmatrix} 5 & 25 & -4 \\ 0 & 25 & 10 \\ 0 & 0 & 10 \\ \end{pmatrix}, \]

    所以令

    \[ P = P_{23}P_{13}P_{12} = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & \frac{3}{5} & \frac{4}{5} \\ - \frac{4}{5} & \frac{12}{25} & - \frac{9}{25} \\ - \frac{3}{5} & - \frac{16}{25} & \frac{12}{25} \\ \end{pmatrix}, T = \begin{pmatrix} 5 & 25 & -4 \\ 0 & 25 & 10 \\ 0 & 0 & 10 \\ \end{pmatrix}, \]

    则 \(PA=T\)

    题目七

    \[ A = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 19 & -34 \\ -2 & -5 & 20 \\ 2 & 8 & 37 \end{pmatrix} \]

    使用Householder reduction实现QR分解

    先构造 \(R_1 = I - 2\frac{u_1 u_1^T}{u_1^T u_1}\)，其中 \(u_1 = (1,-2,2)^T - 3(1,0,0)^T\)

    \[ R_1 = \begin{pmatrix} \frac{1}{3} & -\frac{2}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ -\frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{2}{3} & \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \\ \end{pmatrix} \]
    \[ R_1A = \begin{pmatrix} 3 & 15 & 0 \\ 0 & -9 & 54 \\ 0 & 12 & 3 \\ \end{pmatrix} \]

    构造 \(\widehat{R_2} = I - 2 \frac{u_2 u_2^T}{u_2^T u_2}\)，其中 \(u_2 = (-9,12)^T - 15 (1,0)^T\)

    \[ R_2 = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & \widehat{R_2} \\ \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & -\frac{3}{5} & \frac{4}{5} \\ 0 & \frac{4}{5} & \frac{3}{5} \\ \end{pmatrix} \]
    \[ R_2 R_1 A = \begin{pmatrix} 3 & 15 & 0 \\ 0 & 15 & -30 \\ 0 & 0 & 45 \end{pmatrix} \]
    \[ P = R_2R_1= \begin{pmatrix} \frac{1}{3}&-\frac{2}{3}&\frac{2}{3}\\ \frac{14}{15}&\frac{1}{3}&-\frac{2}{15}\\ -\frac{2}{15}&\frac{2}{3}&\frac{11}{15} \end{pmatrix}, R = \begin{pmatrix} 3 & 15 & 0 \\ 0 & 15 & -30 \\ 0 & 0 & 45 \\ \end{pmatrix} \]

    由 \(PA=R\) 可以得到 \(A=P^{-1}R\)

    \[ Q = P^{-1} = P^T = \begin{pmatrix}\frac{1}{3}&\frac{14}{15}&-\frac{2}{15}\\ \:\:-\frac{2}{3}&\frac{1}{3}&\frac{2}{3}\\ \:\:\frac{2}{3}&-\frac{2}{15}&\frac{11}{15}\end{pmatrix} \]

    则 \(A=QR\)

    使用Givens reduction实现QR分解

    由矩阵A可以得出

    \[ P_{12}= \begin{pmatrix} \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}} & -\frac{2}{\sqrt{5}} & 0 \\ \frac{2}{\sqrt{5}}& \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}} & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{pmatrix}, P_{13}= \begin{pmatrix} \frac{\sqrt{5}}{3} & 0 & \frac{2}{3} \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \\ -\frac{2}{3} & 0 & \frac{\sqrt{5}}{3} \end{pmatrix}, \]

    则：

    \[ P_{13}P_{12} A = \begin{pmatrix} 3 & 15 & 0 \\ 0 & \frac{33}{\sqrt{5}} & -\frac{48}{\sqrt{5}} \\ 0 & -\frac{6}{\sqrt{5}} & \frac{111}{\sqrt{5}} \\ \end{pmatrix}, \]
    \[ P_{23}= \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & \frac{33}{15\sqrt{5}} & -\frac{6}{15\sqrt{5}} \\ 0 & \frac{6}{15\sqrt{5}} & \frac{33}{15\sqrt{5}} \end{pmatrix} \]
    \[ P_{23}P_{13}P_{12} A = \begin{pmatrix} 3 & 15 & 0 \\ 0 & 15 & -30 \\ 0 & 0 & 45 \\ \end{pmatrix} \]

    则可以记

    \[ P = P_{23}P_{13}P_{12} = \begin{pmatrix} \frac{1}{3}&-\frac{2}{3}&\frac{2}{3}\\ \frac{14}{15}&\frac{1}{3}&-\frac{2}{15}\\ -\frac{2}{15}&\frac{2}{3}&\frac{11}{15} \end{pmatrix}, R = \begin{pmatrix} 3 & 15 & 0 \\ 0 & 15 & -30 \\ 0 & 0 & 45 \\ \end{pmatrix} \]

    则 \(PA=R\)，即\(A = P^{-1}R\)

    \[ Q=P^{-1}=P^T = \begin{pmatrix}\frac{1}{3}&\frac{14}{15}&-\frac{2}{15}\\ \:\:-\frac{2}{3}&\frac{1}{3}&\frac{2}{3}\\ \:\:\frac{2}{3}&-\frac{2}{15}&\frac{11}{15}\end{pmatrix} \]

    可以得到QR分解 \(A=QR\)
